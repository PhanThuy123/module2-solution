Drink

Milk tea

Milk tea is known as a drink made from two basic ingredients: tea and milk. Each type of milk tea will have differences in origin, ingredients, preparation method, ratio formula as well as other ingredients. In Vietnam, milk tea recipes from Taiwan with pearl toppings are the most popular milk teas today

Yogurt

Yogurt is fresh cow's milk or milk mixed according to a fresh formula and fermented with bacteria strains that are beneficial to the intestinal system. The main process that occurs in yogurt fermentation is lactose sugar converted into glucose sugar and then converted into pyruvic acid

Coffee

Coffee is a drink made from roasted coffee beans. This is the finished product of ripe coffee cherries picked from the coffee tree. This drink has a strong bitter taste, helping the drinker stay awake. This plant grows mainly in red basalt soils, places near the equator. There are two commonly grown types
